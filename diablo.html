Lamborghini Diablo

The beast that brought Lamborghini into the modern era of supercars.

⬅ Back to Main

Launched in the 1990s, the Diablo was Lamborghini’s first 200+ mph production car. With extreme styling and roaring V12 power, it pushed the envelope of performance and presence.
